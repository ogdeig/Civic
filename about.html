About
Civic Threat is a dashboard that helps people find and follow public conversations in one place. We collect publicly available posts and embeds submitted by the community and organize them by platform and category.
Our goal: make it easier to compare claims, share sources, and participate in respectful discussion. This site is not affiliated with Facebook/Meta or any other platform.
Submissions: People can submit public post embeds for review. Approved submissions appear in the browse pages. We do not require accounts to browse.
